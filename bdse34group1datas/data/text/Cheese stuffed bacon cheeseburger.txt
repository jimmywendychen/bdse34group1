Cheese stuffed bacon cheeseburger 
Ingredients:
8 oz. Ground Beef
2 slices Bacon, pre-cooked
1 oz. Mozzarella Cheese
2 oz. Cheddar Cheese
1 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Pepper
1 tsp. Cajun Seasoning
1 tbsp. Butter
Method:
STEP 1
Season ground beef with all of the spices and mix together lightly.
STEP 2
Prepare the cheese by cubing 1 oz. Mozzarella, and slicing 2 oz. of Cheddar.
STEP 3
Form rough patties with the ground beef and place mozzarella inside, enclosing the cheese with the beef.
STEP 4
Heat 1 tbsp. butter (per burger) in a pan, and wait until bubbling and hot.
STEP 5
Add burger to the pan.
STEP 6
Cover with a cloche and let cook for 2-3 minutes.
STEP 7
Flip the burger and place cheddar cheese on top. Place cloche over the top again and let cook until desired temperature is reached, about 1-2 minutes more.
STEP 8
Chop bacon slice in half and place over the top of the burger. Enjoy!
Nutrients:  
1. 614 Calories
2. 51 Fats
3. 1.5g Net Carbs
4. 33g Protein
