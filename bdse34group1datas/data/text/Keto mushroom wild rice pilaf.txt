Keto mushroom wild rice pilaf
Ingredients:
1 cup Hemp Seeds
2 tbsp. Butter
3 medium Mushrooms
1/4 cup Sliced Almonds
1/2 cup Chicken Broth
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/4 tsp. Dried Parsley
Salt and Pepper to Taste
Method:
STEP 1
Wash and slice mushrooms into small chunks.
STEP 2
Add butter to a pan over medium heat and let melt and bubble. Once bubbling, add sliced almonds and mushrooms to the pan.
STEP 3
Once mushrooms are soft, add hemp seeds to the pan. Mix together well.
STEP 4
Add chicken broth and seasoning to the pan and stir together well. Turn down the heat to medium-low and let the chicken broth simmer and be absorbed.
STEP 5
Once you’re happy with the consistency, turn the pan off and dish out!
STEP 6
This goes great with chicken dishes, but can be used with almost anything.
Nutrients:  
1. 325 Calories
2. 26.3 Fats
3. 1.3g Net Carbs
4. 14.8g Protein
