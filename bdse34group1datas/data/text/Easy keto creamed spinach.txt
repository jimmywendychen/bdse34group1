Easy keto creamed spinach 
Ingredients:
10 oz. Frozen Spinach
3 tbsp. Parmesan Cheese
3 tbsp. Cream Cheese
2 tbsp. Sour Cream
1/4 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/4 tsp. Onion Powder
Salt and Pepper to Taste
Method:
STEP 1
Defrost frozen spinach in the microwave until warmed through, usually about 6-7 minutes.
STEP 2
Heat a pan on the stove to medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, add the spinach and let some of the water boil off. Season the spinach here and mix together.
STEP 3
Add cream cheese and stir together until cream cheese is melted.
STEP 4
Add sour cream and mix together. Turn the pan down to low heat at this point.
STEP 5
Finally, add parmesan cheese and stir until the creamed spinach thickens.
STEP 6
Serve up! It goes great with just about anything!
Nutrients:  
1. 157 Calories
2. 13.3 Fats
3. 2g Net Carbs
4. 5.7g Protein
