Bacon cheese burger casserole 
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef (80/20)
3 slices Bacon
1/2 cup Almond Flour
265g Cauliflower, riced (~3 cups chopped)
1 tbsp. Psyllium Husk Powder
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Onion Powder
2 tbsp. Reduced Sugar
Ketchup
1 tbsp. Dijon Mustard
2 tbsp. Mayonnaise
3 large Eggs
4 oz. Cheddar Cheese (2 oz. inside, 2 oz. on top)
Salt and Pepper to Taste
Method:
STEP 1
Pre-heat oven to 350F. Rice cauliflower in food processor. Add dry ingredients and mix together.
STEP 2
Process bacon and ground beef in food processor until crumbly and slightly pasty. Cook over medium-high, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
STEP 3
Shred the cheese while meat is cooking. Once the meat is done, combine all ingredients in a large bowl and add 2 oz. (half) of the cheddar cheese.
STEP 4
Add eggs, mayo, ketchup, and mustard to the mixture. Using a fork or your hands, mix everything together well.
STEP 5
Press mixture into a 9×9 baking pan lined with parchment paper. Then, top with 2 oz. (the other half) of the cheddar cheese.
STEP 6
Place on top rack and bake for 25-30 minutes. For additional crisp on top, broil 2-3 minutes or until browned.
STEP 7
Remove from oven and let cool for 5-10 minutes.
STEP 8
Slice, and serve with additional toppings. Ideas: mustard, extra reduced sugar ketchup, and pickes.
Nutrients: 
1. 478 Calories
2. 35.5 Fats
3. 3.6g Net Carbs
4. 32.2g Protein
