Keto tater tot nachos (aka totchos)
Ingredients:
2 servings Keto Tater Tots
6 oz. Ground Beef (80/20), cooked
2 oz. Cheddar Cheese, shredded
2 tbsp. Sour Cream
6 Black Olives, sliced
1 tbsp. Salsa
1/2 medium Jalapeno Pepper, sliced
Method:
STEP 1
In a small casserole dish or mini cast iron skillet, lay down 9-10 keto tater tots.
STEP 2
Add 1/2 ground beef, and 1/2 of the shredded cheese. Start the second layer with less tater tots, 1/2 of the remaining beef, and 1/2 of the remaining cheese. Repeat with the last of the tater tots. Broil in the oven for 4-5 minutes so that the cheese melts.
STEP 3
Serve with jalapenos, sour cream, black olives, and salsa. Enjoy!
Nutrients:  
1. 637 Calories
2. 52.5 Fats
3. 5.5g Net Carbs
4. 32.3g Protein
