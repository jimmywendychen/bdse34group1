Thai peanut shrimp curry 
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. Green Curry Paste
1 cup Vegetable Stock
1 cup Coconut Milk
6 oz. Pre-cooked Shrimp
5 oz. Broccoli Florets
3 tbsp. Cilantro, chopped
2 tbsp. Coconut Oil
1 tbsp. Peanut Butter
1 tbsp. Soy Sauce(or coconut aminos)
Juice of 1/2 Lime
1 medium Spring Onion, chopped
1 tsp. Crushed Roasted Garlic
1 tsp. Minced Ginger
1 tsp. Fish Sauce
1/2 tsp. Turmeric
1/4 tsp. Xanthan Gum
1/2 cup Sour Cream(for topping)
Method:
STEP 1
Start by adding 2 tbsp. coconut oil in a pan over medium heat.
STEP 2
When the coconut oil is melted and the pan is hot, add the 1 tsp. roasted garlic, 1 tsp. minced ginger, and 1 spring onion (chopped). Allow to cook for about a minute, then add 1 tbsp. green curry paste, and 1/2 tsp. turmeric.
STEP 3
Add 1 tbsp. soy sauce (or coconut aminos), 1 tsp. fish sauce, and 1 tbsp. peanut butter to the pan and mix together well.
STEP 4
Add 1 cup of vegetable stock and 1 cup of coconut milk (from the carton). Stir well and then add another 1 tbsp. green curry paste.
STEP 5
Let simmer for a few minutes. In the mean time, measure out 6 oz. precooked shrimp.
STEP 6
Add 1/4 tsp. xanthan gum to the curry and mix well.
STEP 7
Once your curry begins thickening up a little bit, add the broccoli florets and stir well.
STEP 8
Chop 3 tbsp. fresh cilantro and add to the pan.
STEP 9
Finally, once you are happy with the consistency of the curry, add the shrimp and lime juice from 1/2 lime, and mix everything together.
STEP 10
Let simmer for a few minutes. Taste and season with salt and pepper if needed.
STEP 11
Serve! You can stir in 1/4 cup of sour cream per serving.
Nutrients:
1. 455 Calories
2. 31.5 Fats
3. 8.9g Net Carbs
4. 27g Protein
